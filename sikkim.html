Sikkim

Explore the breathtaking beauty and rich cultural heritage of Sikkim.

4 Days of Adventure in Sikkim

Day 1 - Exploring Gangtok

  • 8:00 AM - Start your adventure with a hearty breakfast in Gangtok.
  • 9:30 AM - Visit the Enchey Monastery, a serene Buddhist site.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the Namgyal Institute of Tibetology and learn about Tibetan culture.
  • 1:00 PM - Enjoy local Sikkimese cuisine for lunch.
  • 2:30 PM - Visit the flower exhibition center for Himalayan flora.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner at a local restaurant with views of the city.

Day 2 - Tsomgo Lake and Baba Mandir

  • 8:00 AM - Breakfast and drive to Tsomgo Lake.
  • 9:30 AM - Explore the beautiful Tsomgo Lake and enjoy yak rides.
  • 12:00 PM - Visit Baba Harbhajan Singh Mandir, a sacred site for Indian Army soldiers.
  • 1:30 PM - Lunch at a local restaurant on the way back to Gangtok.
  • 3:00 PM - Visit the Directorate of Handicrafts & Handloom for local handicrafts.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner and overnight stay in Gangtok.

Day 3 - Lachung and Yumthang Valley

  • 6:00 AM - Early breakfast and drive to Lachung.
  • 10:00 AM - Explore the stunning Yumthang Valley, known as the Valley of Flowers.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch in Lachung village with traditional Sikkimese cuisine.
  • 2:30 PM - Visit the Lachung Monastery and learn about local culture.
  • 7:00 PM - Return to Gangtok for dinner and rest.

Day 4 - Ravangla and Buddha Park

  • 8:00 AM - Breakfast and drive to Ravangla.
  • 10:00 AM - Visit Buddha Park and enjoy panoramic views of the Himalayas.
  • 12:30 PM - Lunch at a local restaurant in Ravangla.
  • 2:00 PM - Visit the Ralong Monastery and explore the serene surroundings.
