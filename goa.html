3 Days of Adventure in Goa

Day 1 - Beaches and Relaxation

  • 8:00 AM - Start your day with breakfast by the beach.
  • 10:00 AM - Relax on the pristine sands of Calangute Beach.
  • 1:00 PM - Enjoy a seafood lunch at a beachside shack.
  • 3:00 PM - Explore the vibrant markets of Anjuna.
  • 7:00 PM - Experience Goan nightlife at a beach club.

Day 2 - Cultural Exploration

  • 9:00 AM - Visit the historic Basilica of Bom Jesus.
  • 11:00 AM - Discover Goan history at the Goa State Museum.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch at a traditional Goan restaurant.
  • 3:00 PM - Explore the spice plantations of Ponda.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner with local Goan cuisine.

Day 3 - Adventure Activities

  • 9:00 AM - Try water sports at Baga Beach.
  • 12:00 PM - Explore the Dudhsagar Waterfalls.
  • 3:00 PM - Relax and enjoy a sunset cruise.
  • 7:00 PM - Farewell dinner with beach views.
