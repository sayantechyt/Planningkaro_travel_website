Rajasthan

Discover the royal heritage and colorful culture of Rajasthan.

5 Days of Exploration in Rajasthan

Day 1 - Jaipur - The Pink City

  • 8:00 AM - Explore the historic Amber Fort.
  • 11:00 AM - Visit City Palace and Jantar Mantar.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with Rajasthani cuisine.
  • 3:00 PM - Shop for handicrafts and textiles.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner with cultural performances.

Day 2 - Udaipur - The City of Lakes

  • 9:00 AM - Visit the majestic City Palace and museum.
  • 11:00 AM - Boat ride on Lake Pichola to Jag Mandir.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with a view of the lake.
  • 3:00 PM - Explore the local markets and art galleries.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner at a rooftop restaurant.

Day 3 - Jodhpur - The Blue City

  • 8:00 AM - Visit Mehrangarh Fort and Jaswant Thada.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the bustling streets of the old city.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with traditional Rajasthani thali.
  • 3:00 PM - Visit Umaid Bhawan Palace and museum.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner with Rajasthani folk music and dance.

Day 4 - Ranthambore - Wildlife Safari

  • 9:00 AM - Morning safari in Ranthambore National Park.
  • 12:00 PM - Lunch at a wildlife resort.
  • 3:00 PM - Afternoon safari for wildlife photography.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner and overnight stay at the resort.

Day 5 - Pushkar - Spiritual Retreat

  • 8:00 AM - Visit the holy Brahma Temple and Pushkar Lake.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the colorful bazaars and street food.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with local Rajasthani flavors.
  • 3:00 PM - Attend the evening aarti at Varaha Ghat.
  • 7:00 PM - Farewell dinner with traditional Rajasthani cuisine.
