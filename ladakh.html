4 Days of Adventure in Ladakh

Day 1 - Cultural Immersion

  • 8:00 AM - Visit ancient monasteries in the morning.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the Leh Palace and local markets.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with traditional Ladakhi cuisine.
  • 3:00 PM - Discover local handicrafts and artifacts.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner with a view of the Himalayas.

Day 2 - Adventure in Nature

  • 9:00 AM - Trek through scenic valleys and high passes.
  • 12:00 PM - Picnic amidst pristine lakes and glaciers.
  • 3:00 PM - Experience Buddhist rituals at Thiksey Monastery.
  • 7:00 PM - Traditional Ladakhi dinner with cultural performances.

Day 3 - High Altitude Exploration

  • 8:00 AM - Drive to Nubra Valley via Khardung La Pass.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the sand dunes of Hunder and camel safari.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with panoramic views of the valley.
  • 3:00 PM - Visit Diskit Monastery and hot springs.
  • 7:00 PM - Dinner with local Ladakhi delicacies.

Day 4 - Spiritual Retreat

  • 9:00 AM - Morning meditation at Shanti Stupa.
  • 11:00 AM - Explore the heritage of Alchi and Likir Monasteries.
  • 1:00 PM - Lunch with organic Ladakhi food.
  • 3:00 PM - Relax and rejuvenate amidst serene landscapes.
  • 7:00 PM - Farewell dinner with Ladakhi cultural performances.
