Darjeeling

Darjeeling is known for its stunning mountain views, tea gardens, and vibrant culture. Key attractions include:

  • Darjeeling Himalayan Railway
  • Tea Plantations
  • Tiger Hill

Sikkim offers stunning landscapes, vibrant culture, and a serene environment. Key attractions include:

  • Yumthang Valley
  • Rumtek Monastery
  • Nathula Pass
  • Gangtok

  • Baga Beach
  • Anjuna Beach
  • Se Cathedral
  • Dudhsagar Falls
  • Fort Aguada
