4 Days of Adventure in Darjeeling

Day 1 - Exploring Darjeeling

  • 8:00 AM - Start your adventure with a hearty breakfast and a view of the mountains.
  • 9:30 AM - Witness the breathtaking sunrise and the Himalayan range, including Mount Everest.
  • 11:00 AM - Enjoy the panoramic views and beautiful landscapes, perfect for outdoor photography.
  • 1:00 PM - Relish local flavors in a colonial ambiance.
  • 2:30 PM - Adventure through the home of the Snow Leopard and Red Panda.
  • 7:00 PM - End your day with delicious local cuisine. Rest up for another day of adventure.

Day 2 - Tea Gardens and Monasteries

  • 8:00 AM - Enjoy breakfast with a view of the lush tea gardens.
  • 9:30 AM - Explore the tea estate and learn about tea production.
  • 12:00 PM - Discover the spiritual side of Darjeeling at one of its oldest monasteries.
  • 1:30 PM - Taste the local Nepali cuisine in a unique Beatles-themed setting.
  • 3:00 PM - Adventure through the terraced gardens and natural waterfalls.
  • 7:00 PM - Experience authentic Tibetan flavors. Recharge for the next day's journey.

Day 3 - Tranquil Trails

  • 8:00 AM - Savor a quiet breakfast with a scenic view.
  • 9:30 AM - Embark on a trekking adventure with stunning views of the eastern Himalayas.
  • 1:00 PM - Enjoy a small homely cafe offering organic dishes.
  • 2:30 PM - Experience peace and tranquility at this exquisite structure with panoramic views.
  • 7:00 PM - Dine in a serene environment with a blend of local and international cuisines.

Day 4 - Colonial Charm

  • 8:00 AM - Enjoy a classic breakfast in a historic colonial setting.
  • 9:30 AM - Take a joy ride on the UNESCO World Heritage site, the 'Toy Train'.
  • 12:30 PM - Explore the wide variety of Himalayan flora and the serene landscape.
  • 1:30 PM - Indulge in a gourmet lunch with a mix of local and international dishes.
  • 3:00 PM - Enjoy the adventure of a hike up to Observatory Hill for panoramic views.
  • 7:00 PM - Relish the best of Southeast Asian cuisine. Rest and rejuvenate for the next day.
